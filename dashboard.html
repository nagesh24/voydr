Common Questions

Is Voydr completely free?

Yes. Voydr is built to help you, not to monetize your attention. There are no premium tiers or hidden fees.

Can I bypass the blocked sites?

The goal is friction, not a prison. Blocks last until midnight, but if you absolutely must access a site, you can remove it from the dashboard.

Does it work on Safari or Edge?

Currently, Voydr is optimized as a Chrome Extension, which also works seamlessly on Brave, Arc, and Edge browsers.

How does it calculate the Focus Score?

Your score is the ratio of time spent on "Productive" sites versus "Distracting" sites, completely customized by you.
